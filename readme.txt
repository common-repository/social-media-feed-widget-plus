== Installation ==

1. Upload the Facebook Feed Plus plugin (`facebook-feed-plus.zip`) to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to the "widgets" area in your admin panel
4. Drag the "F.B. Feed Plus" widget into your sidebar
5. Add your Facebook Page URL to the URL area and your good to go!
6. Contact us if you have any problems, we are always looking for feedback
